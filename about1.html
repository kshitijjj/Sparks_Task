About  Us

The Sparks Charitable Organization is an organization which basically works for the welfare of the people The key objectives of our organization is to help the people who are not very financially strong and because of which they failed to get the proper education , healthy diet and many other basic needs .So Our organization helps them by raising funds for them.We give our best to fulfill their needs and essential requirements such as providing them a healthy diet,making them aware about the society and providing them a quality education.Our organization is a non profit organization.But for making it revolutionary , we require your support and if you will also contribute in this social cause it would be great and many different problems of our society will come to an end .So,this was a breif detail of our Organization.
